About the Hall

Founded in 2005, the marriage hall has been a witness to over 500 weddings, receptions, familly functions, corporate events to date. An ideal location, combined with various amenities makes GSN mahal would serve as the perfect platform for your event.

Amenities

Two halls with different sizes available to specifically cater to your needs

Main Hall

A large hall with a capacity of 750 people with a dining hall capacity of 300 people. A fully-equipped kitchen to serve 2000 is also present. 

Mini-Hall

A medium-sized hall with a capacity of 300 people with a dining hall capacity of 100 people. A fully-equipped kitchen to serve 800 is also present.

Common Facilities

 

Carparking

Enough space within the premises to house 200 cars. Additional space can be arranged on request.

Bedrooms

Twenty-two rooms available with attached toilets. Both AC and Non-AC options are available.

Power Backup

Two generators available with enough capacity to power a 10 hour function without exteral power

Temple

Vinayagar temple is available within the premises. Priests can be arranged on-request.

Convenient Location

On the trichy road, the hall is only a 30 mins drive from the city. Easily accessible as it is present on the main road.
